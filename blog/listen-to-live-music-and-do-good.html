Listen to Live Music and Do Good

April 29, 2025

Music has a unique power to bring people together, elevate our spirits, and create memorable experiences. In Singapore, the live music scene has been steadily growing, with venues like Simply Live, Simply Jazz, and Retroholics providing spaces for talented musicians to perform and for music lovers to enjoy great entertainment in an intimate setting.

But did you know that when you visit these venues, you're not just supporting local music and having a great time – you can also be part of something even more meaningful?

Tinbox Group: Music with a Heart

Tinbox Group, which owns popular live music bars Simply Live, Simply Jazz, and Retroholics, has made giving back to the community a core part of their business model. Through regular donation campaigns, they invite customers to contribute to various worthy causes, with Tinbox Group matching these donations dollar for dollar.

This 1-to-1 matching approach means that your donation goes twice as far. It's a powerful example of how businesses can amplify the generosity of their customers and make a bigger impact together. Their charitable initiatives include donation drives through Give.Asia, which supports important causes in the community.

In the Spirit of Belanja

This initiative by Tinbox Group perfectly aligns with the Belanja Eat philosophy – creating a culture of generosity and mutual support. Just as Belanja Eat encourages people to "treat" others to meals and support local hawkers, Tinbox Group is inviting music lovers to enjoy great performances while also making a positive difference in the community.

So the next time you're looking for an evening of entertainment, consider visiting one of the Tinbox Group venues. You'll enjoy exceptional live music while having the opportunity to contribute to meaningful causes. It's the perfect example of how we can combine leisure activities with giving back – supporting both our local arts scene and the broader community.
